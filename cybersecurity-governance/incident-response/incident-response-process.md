# Incident Response Process

## Overview

Incident response ensures that organizations detect, respond to, and recover from cybersecurity incidents efficiently.

---

## Typical Incident Response Phases

1. Preparation
2. Detection
3. Analysis
4. Containment
5. Eradication
6. Recovery
7. Post-incident review

---

## Governance Considerations

Effective incident response requires:

- defined roles and responsibilities
- clear escalation paths
- communication protocols
- documentation procedures

---

## Regulatory Considerations

Certain incidents must be reported to authorities under frameworks such as:

- NIS2
- GDPR
- DORA

Organizations must maintain documentation of incidents and response actions.
