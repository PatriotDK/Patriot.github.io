# AI in Compliance

## Overview

AI may support compliance work by helping process, sort, summarize, and identify patterns in large volumes of information.

Possible use cases include:

- document review support
- policy comparison
- regulatory change monitoring
- contract analysis assistance
- compliance workflow support

---

## Potential Benefits

- faster review of large document sets
- improved efficiency
- better information retrieval
- support for documentation and structuring

---

## Key Risks

- inaccurate outputs
- over-reliance on generated summaries
- lack of traceability
- confidentiality concerns
- insufficient legal review

---

## Governance Considerations

Before using AI in compliance functions, organizations should consider:

- approval processes
- data sensitivity
- human review requirements
- documentation obligations
- third-party vendor risk
