# AI Governance Principles

## Overview

AI governance provides the structures, responsibilities, and controls needed to ensure that AI systems are used responsibly and effectively.

---

## Core Principles

### Lawfulness
AI systems should comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

### Accountability
Organizations must define who is responsible for decisions related to AI development, deployment, approval, and oversight.

### Transparency
Users and stakeholders should understand when AI is being used and what role it plays in decisions.

### Human Oversight
Important decisions should not be left entirely to automated systems without meaningful review or intervention where necessary.

### Risk Management
AI systems should be assessed for legal, operational, security, and reputational risk.

### Security and Robustness
AI systems should be resilient, monitored, and protected against misuse or manipulation.

### Documentation
Organizations should maintain records that explain how AI systems are selected, used, monitored, and reviewed.

---

## Practical Importance

Good governance helps organizations:

- reduce regulatory risk
- improve trust
- support internal accountability
- ensure more responsible deployment of AI
